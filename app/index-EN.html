Nearby

The main module of the ZONTO ecosystem. This module allows you to see geotargeted objects at the user location.

The map shows cafes, restaurants, shops, beauty salons, fitness clubs, goods and services located within walking distance from the user. Also, with the help of geolocation, you can find opportunities of leasing living and office accommodations: hotels, hostels, apartments, rooms and much more.

When geolocation is turned on, the user receives useful information, which is selected according to personal movements (for example, more convenient routes and current search results).
